Welcome

Delicious freebies is one of the country’s most celebrated restaurants; the creation of leading Indian restaurant group, Fink, and Executive Chef Jayasri kandregula. The restaurant is an organic space reflective of nature inspired cuisines. The interplay of textures and colour brings life and a vibrance that embraces the restaurant’s place in the dress circle of South India. An ode to the Southeren landscape, from the vast ocean floor, to the cracked bark of a paperbark tree, every detail from the ground up has been thoughtfully considered.
